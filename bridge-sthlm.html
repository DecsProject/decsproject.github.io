Stockholm Nightlife —
Geospatial Study

Forty years of Stockholm's entertainment landscape — mapped, layered, and interrogated. Tracking how bars, clubs, cinemas, and cultural venues have appeared, shifted, and disappeared across the city from 1985 to today. The centrepiece is an interactive map that lets you move through time and watch the city's nightlife geography evolve.

Three eras
1985 – 1999
Analog era
Dance restaurants, rock clubs, city cinemas, and arcade halls — before the internet changed everything.
2000 – 2009
Digitalisation
Internet cafés, the Stureplan era, multiplex cinemas, and the first wave of large-format nightclubs.
2010 – 2025
Mobile era
Craft beer bars, escape rooms, food courts, e-sport arenas, and coworking cafés reshape the map.
